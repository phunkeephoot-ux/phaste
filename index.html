The Gentle Break

Best for: Beginners & Sensitive Stomachs

  • Step 1: Warm water with lemon (1 cup)
  • Step 2: 1 cup bone broth or vegetable broth
  • Step 3: Small portion of fermented food (sauerkraut)

The Performance Break

Best for: Athletes & Muscle Gain

  • Step 1: BCAA drink (optional)
  • Step 2: Lean protein source (Chicken/White Fish)
  • Step 3: Fast-digesting carbs (White rice)
